## Mobile Sora App Video Creation Tutorial

Step 1: Open Sora App
Open the Sora App on your mobile phone. Tap “Create Video” to start creating a video.

![02](/presentation/src/assets/page2/images/02.PNG)

Step 2: Enter Prompt
Type what you want to generate. The better the description, the better the result.


![03](/presentation/src/assets/page2/images/03.PNG)

Step 3: Choose Video Settings
Adjust video duration, resolution, and screen orientation if needed.

Step 4: Generate Video
Tap “Generate”. You can leave the app during creation.

![05](/presentation/src/assets/page2/images/05.PNG)

Step 5: Preview & Save
Download the video to your phone or share it on social platforms


![04](/presentation/src/assets/page2/images/04.PNG)

Prompt Writing Tips
Structure: Subject + Action + Scene + Camera + Style + Duration + Ratio
Example: A golden retriever running on the beach at sunset, cinematic lighting, follow shot, 8 seconds, 16:9
You need a US Apple account, as this software is region-locked.

![01](/presentation/src/assets/page2/images/01.PNG)

Summary
Using Sora App, anyone can easily create high-quality AI videos with only a mobile phone.
